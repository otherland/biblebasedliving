'How Old is God According to the Bible'

Posted on 24 May 2024

This article has been fact-checked and verified for accuracy.

How Old is God According to the Bible?

According to the Bible, God has no age as He exists eternally, without beginning or end. Scripture states "Before the mountains were brought forth... from everlasting to everlasting you are God" (Psalm 90:2). As the Creator of time itself, God exists outside temporal limitations. Biblical names like "I AM" (Exodus 3:14) and "Alpha and Omega" (Revelation 1:8) emphasize His eternal nature.

Direct Biblical Statements About God's Age

  1. Before Creation
  2. Psalm 90:2: "Before the mountains were brought forth, or ever you had formed the earth and the world, from everlasting to everlasting you are God"
  3. Proverbs 8:22-23: "The Lord possessed me at the beginning of his way, before his works of old. I was set up from everlasting, from the beginning, before the earth was"

  4. John 17:5: "And now, Father, glorify me in your own presence with the glory that I had with you before the world existed"

  5. Eternal Existence

  6. 1 Timothy 1:17: "To the King eternal, immortal, invisible, the only God, be honor and glory forever and ever"
  7. Deuteronomy 33:27: "The eternal God is your dwelling place, and underneath are the everlasting arms"

  8. Isaiah 40:28: "The Lord is the everlasting God, the Creator of the ends of the earth"

  9. No Beginning or End

  10. Revelation 1:8: "I am the Alpha and the Omega, says the Lord God, who is and who was and who is to come, the Almighty"
  11. Revelation 22:13: "I am the Alpha and the Omega, the first and the last, the beginning and the end"
  12. Hebrews 7:3: "Having neither beginning of days nor end of life"

Understanding Time in the Bible

God's Creation of Time

The Bible shows God existed before time and created it:

  1. Creation of Time Markers
  2. Genesis 1:1: "In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth"

  3. Genesis 1:14: "Let there be lights in the expanse of the heavens to separate the day from the night. And let them be for signs and for seasons, and for days and years"

  4. God's Relationship to Time

  5. 2 Peter 3:8: "With the Lord one day is as a thousand years, and a thousand years as one day"
  6. Psalm 102:24-27: "Your years endure throughout all generations... they will perish, but you will remain"

Biblical Timeline and God's Existence

The Bible presents a clear timeline showing God's existence:

  1. Before Creation
  2. God exists eternally
  3. No beginning point mentioned

  4. Active before universe creation

  5. Creation Week

  6. God creates time (Genesis 1:1-5)
  7. Establishes day/night cycle

  8. Sets up celestial time markers

  9. Throughout History

  10. Present in all biblical events
  11. Consistently described as eternal

  12. Never changes or ages

  13. Future Eternity

  14. Revelation 1:4: "who is and who was and who is to come"
  15. Psalm 102:27: "but you are the same, and your years have no end"
  16. Exists beyond end of current creation

God's Self-Revelation About His Age

The Meaning of "I AM"

When God revealed Himself to Moses in Exodus 3:14:

  1. Hebrew Name Explanation
  2. YHWH (יהוה) means "I AM WHO I AM"
  3. Indicates eternal present existence

  4. Shows existence independent of time

  5. Biblical Usage

  6. Used over 6,800 times in Old Testament
  7. Consistently represents eternal nature
  8. Connected to God's unchanging character

Other Biblical Names Indicating God's Ageless Nature

  1. El Olam (עוֹלָם אֵל)
  2. Meaning: The Everlasting God
  3. First used in Genesis 21:33

  4. Emphasizes God's eternal existence

  5. Ancient of Days

  6. Used in Daniel 7:9
  7. Represents eternal authority
  8. Shows God's existence before time

Common Questions About God's Age

Does God Have a Beginning?

The Bible clearly teaches that God has no beginning:

  1. Biblical Evidence
  2. Never mentions God's beginning
  3. Describes God before creation

  4. Uses terms indicating eternal existence

  5. Scriptural Support

  6. Psalm 93:2: "Your throne is established from of old; you are from everlasting"
  7. Isaiah 43:13: "From eternity I am He"

Can We Calculate God's Age?

The Bible shows why God's age cannot be calculated:

  1. Beyond Time
  2. Exists outside time framework
  3. Created time itself

  4. Not bound by temporal limits

  5. Biblical Perspective

  6. No starting point to measure from
  7. Eternal nature transcends measurement
  8. Exists in eternal present

Biblical Summary of God's Age

According to Scripture:

  1. God is Eternal
  2. No beginning
  3. No end

  4. Outside time itself

  5. Biblical Proof

  6. Consistent testimony throughout Scripture
  7. Multiple divine names indicating eternality

  8. Clear statements about God's timeless nature

  9. Key Truth

  10. God doesn't have an age in human terms
  11. Exists eternally past and future
  12. Created and transcends time itself

This biblical understanding shows that asking "How old is God?" reflects our time-bound perspective. The Bible consistently teaches that God exists eternally, without beginning or end, transcending the very concept of age itself.
